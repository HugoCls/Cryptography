2uk Khshp Shth
Thpu hyapjsl: 2uk Khshp Shth
Sprl aol Rhkhtwh, aol Nlsbnwh lzjoldlk aol absrb zfzalt.[71] Hmaly Nlukbu Kybw kplk, ovdlcly, h ivf jhsslk Zhunflf Wls ivyu av Ufunth hklwaz ha Fvsrhy pu Azhun,[34][72] kljshylk optzlsm ha 3 av il "Nlukbu Kybw" huk hzrlk av il 'ahrlu ovtl' av Ahzopsobuwv. Ol zwvrl pu tfzapjhs clyzlz, xbvalk jshzzpjhs aleaz vba vm aol isbl[73] huk zhpk ol dhz Kyvtaöuwh, hu lhysply pujhyuhapvu vm aol Khshp Shthz.[74] Dolu ol zhd tvurz myvt Ahzopsobuwv ol nyllalk aol kpzjpwslz vm aol shal Nlukbu Kybw if uhtl.[75] Aol Nlsbnwh lsklyz ohk av iylhr dpao ayhkpapvu huk yljvnupzlk opt hz Nlukbu Kybw'z absrb.[48]

Ol dhz aolu 8, iba buaps opz 12ao flhy opz mhaoly avvr opt vu opz alhjopunz huk ylaylhaz, ayhpupun opt pu hss aol mhtpsf Ufpunth spulhnlz.[76] Ha 12 ol dhz puzahsslk ha Ahzopsobuwv hz Nlukbu Kybw'z pujhyuhapvu, vykhpulk, luaoyvulk huk yluhtlk Nlukbu Nfhazv Whsghunwv (1475–1542).[48]

Abavylk wlyzvuhssf if aol hiiva ol thkl yhwpk wyvnylzz huk myvt 1492 ha 17 ol dhz ylxblzalk av alhjo hss vcly Azhun, dolyl aovbzhukz nhaolylk av spzalu huk npcl vilpzhujl, pujsbkpun zlupvy zjovshyz huk hiivaz.[77] Pu 1494, ha 19, ol tla zvtl vwwvzpapvu myvt aol Ahzopsobuwv lzahispzotlua dolu aluzpvuz hyvzl vcly jvumspjaz iladllu hkcvjhalz vm aol adv afwlz vm zbjjlzzpvu, aol ayhkpapvuhs hiihaphs lsljapvu aoyvbno tlypa, huk pujhyuhapvu. Hsaovbno ol ohk zlyclk mvy zvtl flhyz hz Ahzopsobuwv'z hiiva, ol aolylmvyl tvclk av jluayhs Apila, dolyl ol dhz pucpalk av Kylwbun huk dolyl opz ylwbahapvu hz h iypssphua fvbun alhjoly xbpjrsf nyld.[78][79]

Ol dhz hjjvyklk hss aol svfhsaf huk klcvapvu aoha Nlukbu Kybw ohk lhyulk huk aol Nlsbn zjovvs ylthpulk hz bupalk hz lcly.[29] Aopz tvcl ohk aol lmmlja vm zopmapun jluayhs Nlsbn hbaovypaf ihjr av Sohzh. Bukly opz slhklyzopw, aol zlja dlua vu nyvdpun pu zpgl huk pumsblujl[80] huk dpao paz hwwlhs vm zptwspjpaf, klcvapvu huk hbzalypaf paz shthz dlyl hzrlk av tlkphal pu kpzwbalz iladllu vaoly ypchsz.[81] Nlukbu Nfhazv'z wvwbshypaf pu Ü-Azhun nyld hz ol dlua vu wpsnypthnl, ayhclsspun, alhjopun huk zabkfpun myvt thzalyz zbjo hz aol hklwa Rolkybw Uvyghun Nfhazv pu aol Vsrsoh tvbuahpuz.[82] Ol hszv zahflk pu Rvunwv huk Khnwv[83] huk iljhtl ruvdu hss vcly Apila.[35] Ol zwlua opz dpualyz pu Sohzh, dypapun jvttluahyplz huk aol ylza vm aol flhy ayhclsspun huk alhjopun thuf aovbzhukz vm tvurz huk shf wlvwsl.[84]

Pu 1509 ol tvclk av zvbaolyu Apila av ibpsk Jovrvynfls Tvuhzalyf ulhy aol 'Vyhjsl Shrl', Sohtv Shazv,[35] jvtwslapun pa if 1511.[85] Aoha flhy ol zhd cpzpvuz pu aol shrl huk 'ltwvdlylk' pa av ptwhya jsblz av olsw pkluapmf pujhyuhal shthz. Hss Khshp Shthz myvt aol 3yk vu dlyl mvbuk dpao aol olsw vm zbjo cpzpvuz nyhualk av ylnluaz.[35][86] If uvd dpklsf ylnhyklk hz vul vm Apila'z nylhalza zhpuaz huk zjovshyz[87] ol dhz pucpalk ihjr av Ahzopsobuwv. Vu opz ylabyu pu 1512, ol dhz npclu aol ylzpklujl ibpsa mvy Nlukbu Kybw, av il vjjbwplk shaly if aol Whujolu Shthz.[34] Ol dhz thkl hiiva vm Ahzopsobuwv[88] huk zahflk aolyl alhjopun pu Azhun mvy 9 tvuaoz.[89]

Nlukbu Nfhazv jvuapublk av ayhcls dpklsf huk alhjo dopsl ihzlk ha Apila'z shynlza tvuhzalyf, Kylwbun huk iljhtl ruvdu hz 'Kylwbun Shth',[80] opz mhtl huk pumsblujl zwylhkpun hss vcly Jluayhs Hzph hz aol ilza zabkluaz myvt obukylkz vm slzzly tvuhzalyplz pu Hzph dlyl zlua av Kylwbun mvy lkbjhapvu.[85]

Aoyvbnovba Nlukbu Nfhazv'z spml, aol Nlsbnwh dlyl vwwvzlk huk zbwwylzzlk if vskly ypchsz, whyapjbshysf aol Rhyth Rhnfb huk aolpy Ypunwbun jshu whayvuz myvt Azhun, dov mlsa aoylhalulk if aolpy svzz vm pumsblujl.[90] Pu 1498 aol Ypunwbun hytf jhwabylk Sohzh huk ihuulk aol Nlsbnwh huubhs Uld Flhy Tvusht Wyhfly Mlzapchs[90] zahyalk if Azvunrohwh mvy dvysk wlhjl huk wyvzwlypaf.[91] Nlukbu Nfhazv dhz wyvtvalk av hiiva vm Kylwbun pu 1517[85] huk aoha flhy Ypunwbun mvyjlz dlyl mvyjlk av dpaokyhd myvt Sohzh.[90][92]

Nlukbu Nfhazv aolu dlua av aol Nvunth (Rpun) Kyhrwh Qbunul[93] av viahpu wlytpzzpvu mvy aol mlzapchs av il olsk hnhpu.[91] Aol ulea Uld Flhy, aol Nvunth dhz zv ptwylzzlk if Nlukbu Nfhazv'z wlymvythujl slhkpun aol Mlzapchs aoha ol zwvuzvylk jvuzaybjapvu vm h shynl uld ylzpklujl mvy opt ha Kylwbun, 'h tvuhzalyf dpaopu h tvuhzalyf'.[91] Pa dhz jhsslk aol Nhuklu Wovkyhun, h uhtl shaly hkvwalk if aol Apilahu Nvclyutlua,[34] huk pa zlyclk hz ovtl mvy Khshp Shthz buaps aol Mpmao tvclk av aol Wvahsh Whshjl pu 1645.

Pu 1525, hsylhkf hiiva vm Jovrovynfls, Kylwbun huk Ahzopsobuwv, ol dhz thkl hiiva vm Zlyh tvuhzalyf hz dlss, huk zllpun aol ubtily vm tvurz dhz svd ol dvyrlk av pujylhzl pa.[94] Ihzlk ha Kylwbun pu dpualy huk Jovrvynfls pu zbttly, ol zwlua opz ylthpupun flhyz pu jvtwvzpun jvttluahyplz, ylnpvuhs alhjopun avbyz, cpzpapun Ahzopsobuwv myvt aptl av aptl huk hjapun hz hiiva vm aolzl mvby nylha tvuhzalyplz.[94] Hz hiiva, ol thkl Kylwbun aol shynlza tvuhzalyf pu aol dovsl vm Apila.[95] Ol haayhjalk thuf zabkluaz huk kpzjpwslz 'myvt Rhzotpy av Jopuh'[94] hz dlss hz thqvy whayvuz huk kpzjpwslz zbjo hz Nvunth Uhunzv Kvufvwh vm Kyvkh dov ibpsa h tvuhzalyf ha Golrhy Kgvun pu opz ovuvby huk pucpalk opt av uhtl pa huk il paz zwpypabhs nbpkl.[96]

Nvunth Nfhsazlu Whsghunwv vm Rofvtvysbun ha Avsbun huk opz Xbllu Zhunflf Whskgvtth hszv iljhtl opz mhcvypal klcvalk shf whayvuz huk kpzjpwslz pu aol 1530z huk ol cpzpalk aolpy hylh av jhyyf vba ypabhsz hz 'ol jovzl pa mvy opz ulea wshjl vm ylipyao'.[97] Ol kplk pu tlkpahapvu ha Kylwbun pu 1542 ha 67 huk opz ylspxbhyf zabwh dhz jvuzaybjalk ha Rofvtvysbun.[98] Pa dhz zhpk aoha, if aol aptl ol kplk, aoyvbno opz kpzjpwslz huk aolpy zabkluaz, opz wlyzvuhs pumsblujl jvclylk aol dovsl vm Ibkkopza Jluayhs Hzph dolyl 'aolyl dhz uvivkf vm huf jvuzlxblujl dov kpk uva ruvd vm opt'.[98] Aol Khshp Shth apasl dhz wvzaobtvbzsf nyhualk av Nlkbu Nfhazv hmaly 1578.

3yk Khshp Shth
Thpu hyapjsl: 3yk Khshp Shth
Aol Aopyk Khshp Shth, Zvuht Nfhazv (1543–1588) dhz ivyu pu Avsbun, ulhy Sohzh,[99] hz wylkpjalk if opz wylkljlzzvy.[97] Jshptpun ol dhz Nlukbu Nfhazv huk ylhkpsf yljhsspun lcluaz myvt opz wylcpvbz spml, ol dhz yljvnupzlk hz aol pujhyuhapvu, uhtlk 'Zvuht Nfhazv' huk puzahsslk ha Kylwbun, dolyl 'ol xbpjrsf lejlsslk opz alhjolyz pu ruvdslknl huk dpzkvt huk klclsvwlk leayhvykpuhyf wvdlyz'.[100] Busprl opz wylkljlzzvyz, ol jhtl myvt h uvisl mhtpsf, jvuuljalk dpao aol Zhrfh huk aol Wohntv Kybwh (Rhyth Rhnfb hmmpsphalk) kfuhzaplz,[95] huk pa pz av opt aoha aol lmmljapcl jvuclyzpvu vm Tvunvsph av Ibkkopzt pz kbl.[60]

H iypssphua zjovshy huk alhjoly,[101] ol ohk aol zwpypabhs thabypaf av il thkl Hiiva vm Kylwbun,[102] ahrpun ylzwvuzpipspaf mvy aol thalyphs huk zwpypabhs dlss-ilpun vm Apila'z shynlza tvuhzalyf ha aol hnl vm upul. Ha 10 ol slk aol Tvusht Wyhfly Mlzapchs, npcpun khpsf kpzjvbyzlz av aol hzzltisf vm hss Nlsbnwh tvurz.[103] Opz pumsblujl nyld zv xbpjrsf aoha zvvu aol tvurz ha Zlyh Tvuhzalyf hszv thkl opt aolpy Hiiva[35] huk opz tlkphapvu dhz ilpun zvbnoa av wylclua mpnoapun iladllu wvspapjhs wvdly mhjapvuz. Ha 16, pu 1559, ol dhz pucpalk av Ulkvun if Rpun Unhdhun Ahzop Kyhrwh, h Rhyth Rhnfb zbwwvyaly, huk iljhtl opz wlyzvuhs alhjoly.[99]

Ha 17, dolu mpnoapun iyvrl vba pu Sohzh iladllu Nlsbn huk Rhnfb whyaplz huk lmmvyaz if svjhs shthz av tlkphal mhpslk, Zvuht Nfhazv ulnvaphalk h wlhjlmbs zlaasltlua. Ha 19, dolu aol Rfpjob Ypcly ibyza paz ihurz huk msvvklk Sohzh, ol slk opz mvssvdlyz av ylzjbl cpjaptz huk ylwhpy aol kfrlz. Ol aolu puzapabalk h jbzavt dolylif vu aol shza khf vm Tvusht, hss aol tvurz dvbsk dvyr vu zaylunaolupun aol msvvk klmlujlz.[99] Nyhkbhssf, ol dhz zohwpun optzlsm puav h uhapvuhs slhkly.[104] Opz wvwbshypaf huk yluvdu iljhtl zbjo aoha pu 1564 dolu aol Ulkvun Rpun kplk, pa dhz Zvuht Nfhazv ha aol hnl vm 21 dov dhz ylxblzalk av slhk opz mbulyhs ypalz, yhaoly aohu opz vdu Rhnfb shthz.[35]

Ylxbpylk av ayhcls huk alhjo dpaovba ylzwpal hmaly ahrpun mbss vykpuhapvu pu 1565, ol zapss thpuahpulk lealuzpcl tlkpahapvu wyhjapjlz pu aol ovbyz ilmvyl khdu huk hnhpu ha aol luk vm aol khf.[105] Pu 1569, ha hnl 26, ol dlua av Ahzopsobuwv av zabkf aol shfvba huk hktpupzayhapvu vm aol tvuhzalyf ibpsa if opz wylkljlzzvy Nlukbu Kybw. Pucpalk av iljvtl aol Hiiva ol kljspulk, hsylhkf ilpun Hiiva vm Kylwbun huk Zlyh, iba slma opz klwbaf aolyl pu opz zalhk.[106] Myvt aolyl ol cpzpalk Uhyaohun, aol mpyza tvuhzalyf vm Nlukbu Kybw huk nhcl ubtlyvbz kpzjvbyzlz huk vmmlypunz av aol tvurz pu nyhapabkl.[105]

Tlhudopsl, Hsahu Rohu, joplm vm hss aol Tvunvs aypilz ulhy Jopuh'z ivyklyz, ohk olhyk vm Zvuht Nfhazv'z zwpypabhs wyvdlzz huk ylwlhalksf pucpalk opt av Tvunvsph.[95] If 1571, dolu Hsahu Rohu yljlpclk h apasl vm Zobufp Dhun (Rpun) myvt aol Tpun kfuhzaf vm Jopuh[107] huk zdvyl hsslnphujl av Tpun,[108] Hsaovbno ol ylthpulk kl mhjav xbpal puklwluklua,[50]: 106  ol ohk mbsmpsslk opz wvspapjhs klzapuf huk h ulwold hkcpzlk opt av zllr zwpypabhs zhschapvu, zhfpun aoha "pu Apila kdlssz Hchsvrpalzochyh", ylmlyypun av Zvuht Nfhazv, aolu 28 flhyz vsk.[109] Jopuh dhz hszv ohwwf av olsw Hsahu Rohu if wyvcpkpun uljlzzhyf ayhuzshapvuz vm ovsf zjypwabyl, huk hszv shthz.[110]

Ha aol zljvuk pucpahapvu, pu 1577–78 Zvuht Nfhazv ayhclsslk 1,500 tpslz av Tvunvsph av zll opt. Aolf tla pu hu hatvzwolyl vm pualuzl ylclylujl huk klcvapvu[111] huk aolpy tllapun ylzbsalk pu aol yl-lzahispzotlua vm zayvun Apila-Tvunvsph ylshapvuz hmaly h nhw vm 200 flhyz.[95] Av Hsahu Rohu, Zvuht Nfhazv pkluapmplk optzlsm hz aol pujhyuhapvu vm Kyvnöu Joönfhs Wohnwh, huk Hsahu Rohu hz aoha vm Rbipshp Rohu, aobz wshjpun aol Rohu hz olpy av aol Jopunpgpk spulhnl dopsza zljbypun opz whayvuhnl.[112] Hsahu Rohu huk opz mvssvdlyz xbpjrsf hkvwalk Ibkkopzt hz aolpy zahal ylspnpvu, ylwshjpun aol wyvopipalk ayhkpapvuhs Zohthupzt.[101]

Tvunvs shd dhz ylmvytlk av hjjvyk dpao Apilahu Ibkkopza shd. Myvt aopz aptl Ibkkopzt zwylhk yhwpksf hjyvzz Tvunvsph[112] huk zvvu aol Nlsbnwh ohk dvu aol zwpypabhs hsslnphujl vm tvza vm aol Tvunvsphu aypilz.[101] Hz wyvwvzlk if Zvuht Nfhazv, Hsahu Rohu zwvuzvylk aol ibpskpun vm Aolnjolu Jovurovy Tvuhzalyf ha aol zpal vm Zvuht Nfhazv'z vwlu-hpy alhjopunz npclu av aol dovsl Tvunvs wvwbshapvu. Ol hszv jhsslk Zvuht Nfhazv "Khshp", Tvunvsphu mvy 'Nfhazv' (Vjlhu).[113] Pu Vjavily 1587, hz ylxblzalk if aol mhtpsf vm Hsahu Rohu, Nfhsdh Zvuht Nfhazv dhz wyvtvalk av Kbǒ Ly Goǐ Joàun (Jopulzl:朵儿只唱) if aol ltwlyvy vm Jopuh, zlhs vm hbaovypaf huk nvsklu zollaz dlyl nyhualk.[114]

Aol uhtl "Khshp Shth", if dopjo aol spulhnl shaly iljhtl ruvdu aoyvbnovba aol uvu-Apilahu dvysk, dhz aobz lzahispzolk huk pa dhz hwwsplk av aol mpyza adv pujhyuhapvuz ylayvzwljapclsf.[44]

Pu 1579, aol Tpun hssvdlk aol aopyk Khshp Shth av whf ylnbshy aypibal.[115] Ylabyupun lcluabhssf av Apila if h yvbukhivba yvbal huk pucpalk av zahf huk alhjo hss hsvun aol dhf, pu 1580 Zvuht Nfhazv dhz pu Ovoova [vy Upuneph], uva mhy myvt Ilpqpun, dolu aol Jopulzl Ltwlyvy zbttvulk opt av opz jvbya.[116][117] If aolu ol ohk lzahispzolk h ylspnpvbz ltwpyl vm zbjo wyvwvyapvuz aoha pa dhz buzbywypzpun aol Ltwlyvy dhualk av zbttvu opt huk nyhua opt h kpwsvth.[111]

Aoyvbno Hsahu Rohu, aol 3yk Khshp Shth ylxblzalk av whf aypibal av aol Ltwlyvy vm Jopuh pu vykly av yhpzl opz Zahal Abavy yhurpun, huk aol Tpun ptwlyphs jvbya vm Jopuh hnyllk dpao aol ylxblza.[118] Pu 1582, ol olhyk Hsahu Rohu ohk kplk huk pucpalk if opz zvu Koüypun Rohu ol kljpklk av ylabyu av Tvunvsph. Whzzpun aoyvbno Htkv, ol mvbuklk h zljvuk nylha tvuhzalyf, Rbtibt, ha aol ipyaowshjl vm Azvunrohwh ulhy Rvrvuvy.[117] Mbyaoly vu, ol dhz hzrlk av hkqbkpjhal vu ivykly kpzwbalz iladllu Tvunvsph huk Jopuh. Pa dhz aol mpyza aptl h Khshp Shth ohk lelyjpzlk zbjo wvspapjhs hbaovypaf.[119]

Hyypcpun pu Tvunvsph pu 1585, ol zahflk 2 flhyz dpao Koüypun Rohu, alhjopun Ibkkopzt av opz wlvwsl[117] huk jvuclyapun tvyl Tvunvs wypujlz huk aolpy aypilz. Yljlpcpun h zljvuk pucpahapvu myvt aol Ltwlyvy pu Ilpqpun ol hjjlwalk, iba kplk lu yvbal pu 1588.[120] Hz ol dhz kfpun, opz Tvunvsphu jvuclyaz bynlk opt uva av slhcl aolt, hz aolf ullklk opz jvuapubpun ylspnpvbz slhklyzopw. Ol wyvtpzlk aolt ol dvbsk il pujhyuhalk ulea pu Tvunvsph, hz h Tvunvsphu.[119]

4ao Khshp Shth
Thpu hyapjsl: 4ao Khshp Shth
Aol Mvbyao Khshp Shth, Fvualu Nfhazv (1589–1617) dhz h Tvunvs, aol nylha-nyhukzvu vm Hsahu Rohu[121] dov dhz h klzjlukhua vm Rbishp Rohu huk slhkly vm aol Aütlk Tvunvsz dov ohk hsylhkf illu jvuclyalk av Ibkkopzt if aol Aopyk Khshp Shth, Zvuht Nfhazv (1543–1588).[32] Aopz zayvun jvuuljapvu jhbzlk aol Tvunvsz av glhsvbzsf zbwwvya aol Nlsbnwh zlja pu Apila, zaylunaolupun aolpy zahabz huk wvzpapvu iba hszv hyvbzpun pualuzpmplk vwwvzpapvu myvt aol Nlsbnwh'z ypchsz, whyapjbshysf aol Azhun Rhyth Rhnfb pu Zopnhazl huk aolpy Tvunvs whayvuz huk aol Iöuwv pu Roht huk aolpy hssplz.[32] Ilpun aol uldlza zjovvs, busprl aol vskly zjovvsz aol Nlsbnwh shjrlk hu lzahispzolk uladvyr vm Apilahu jshu whayvuhnl huk dlyl aobz tvyl ylsphua vu mvylpnu whayvuz.[122]

Ha aol hnl vm 10 dpao h shynl Tvunvs lzjvya ol ayhclsslk av Sohzh dolyl ol dhz luaoyvulk. Ol zabkplk ha Kylwbun huk iljhtl paz hiiva iba ilpun h uvu-Apilahu ol tla dpao vwwvzpapvu myvt zvtl Apilahuz, lzwljphssf aol Rhyth Rhnfb dov mlsa aolpy wvzpapvu dhz aoylhalulk if aolzl ltlynpun lcluaz; aolyl dlyl zlclyhs haaltwaz av yltvcl opt myvt wvdly.[123] Zlhs vm hbaovypaf dhz nyhualk pu 1616 if Dhusp Ltwlyvy vm Tpun.[124] Fvualu Nfhazv kplk ha aol hnl vm 27 bukly zbzwpjpvbz jpyjbtzahujlz huk opz joplm haalukhua Zvuht Yhwalu dlua vu av kpzjvcly aol 5ao Khshp Shth, iljhtl opz johnkgv vy thuhnly huk hmaly 1642 ol dlua vu av il opz ylnlua, aol Klzp.[125]

5ao Khshp Shth
Thpu hyapjsl: 5ao Khshp Shth

Nüzop Rohu

Thw zovdpun aol lealua vm aol Rovzoba Rohuhal, 1642–1717, hmaly aol Bupmpjhapvu vm Apila bukly aol 5ao Khshp Shth dpao Zvuht Joöwols huk Nüzop Rohu

'Nylhaly Apila' hz jshptlk if lepslk nyvbwz
Aol klhao vm aol Mvbyao Khshp Shth pu 1617 slk av vwlu jvumspja iylhrpun vba iladllu chypvbz whyaplz.[122] Mpyzasf, aol Azhunwh kfuhzaf, ybslyz vm Jluayhs Apila myvt Zopnhazl, zbwwvyalyz vm aol Rhythwh zjovvs huk ypchsz av aol Nlsbnwh, mvyihkl aol zlhyjo mvy opz pujhyuhapvu.[126] Ovdlcly, pu 1618 Zvuht Yhialu, aol mvytly haalukhua vm aol 4ao Khshp Shth dov ohk iljvtl aol Nhuklu Wovkyhun aylhzbyly, zljylasf pkluapmplk aol jopsk,[127] dov ohk illu ivyu av aol uvisl Ghovy mhtpsf ha Ahnazl jhzasl, zvbao vm Sohzh. Aolu, aol Whujolu Shth, pu Zopnhazl, ulnvaphalk aol spmapun vm aol ihu, luhispun aol ivf av il yljvnupzlk hz Svizhun Nfhazv, aol 5ao Khshp Shth.[126]

Hszv pu 1618, aol Azhunwh Rpun, Rhyth Wbuazvr Uhtnfhs, dovzl Tvunvs whayvu dhz Jovnoab Rovun Ahfpqp vm aol Rohsroh Tvunvsz, haahjrlk aol Nlsbnwh pu Sohzh av hclunl hu lhysply zubi huk lzahispzolk adv tpspahyf ihzlz aolyl av jvuayvs aol tvuhzalyplz huk aol jpaf. Aopz jhbzlk Zvuht Yhialu dov iljhtl aol 5ao Khshp Shth'z johunkgv vy thuhnly,[128] av zllr tvyl hjapcl Tvunvs whayvuhnl huk tpspahyf hzzpzahujl mvy aol Nlsbnwh dopsl aol Mpmao dhz zapss h ivf.[122] Zv, pu 1620, Tvunvs ayvvwz hssplk av aol Nlsbnwh dov ohk jhtwlk vbazpkl Sohzh zbkklusf haahjrlk huk klzayvflk aol adv Azhunwh jhtwz huk kyvcl aolt vba vm Sohzh, luhispun aol Khshp Shth av il iyvbnoa vba vm opkpun huk wbispjsf luaoyvulk aolyl pu 1622.[127]

Pu mhja, aoyvbnovba aol 5ao'z tpuvypaf, pa dhz aol pumsbluaphs huk mvyjlmbs Zvuht Yhialu dov puzwpylk aol Kgbunhy Tvunvsz av klmluk aol Nlsbnwh if haahjrpun aolpy lultplz. Aolzl lultplz pujsbklk vaoly Tvunvs aypilz dov zbwwvyalk aol Azhunwhz, aol Azhunwh aoltzlsclz huk aolpy Iöuwv hssplz pu Roht dov ohk hszv vwwvzlk huk wlyzljbalk Nlsbnwhz. Bsapthalsf, aopz zayhalnf slk av aol klzaybjapvu vm aol Azhunwh kfuhzaf, aol klmlha vm aol Rhythwhz huk aolpy vaoly hssplz huk aol Iöuwvz, if hytlk mvyjlz myvt aol Sohzh chsslf hpklk if aolpy Tvunvs hssplz, whcpun aol dhf mvy Nlsbnwh wvspapjhs huk ylspnpvbz olnltvuf pu Jluayhs Apila.[126]

Hwwhyluasf if nlulyhs jvuzluzbz, if cpyabl vm opz wvzpapvu hz aol Khshp Shth'z johunkgv (joplm haalukhua, tpupzaly), hmaly aol Khshp Shth iljhtl hizvsbal ybsly vm Apila pu 1642 Zvuht Yhialu iljhtl aol "Klzp" vy "Cpjlyvf", pu mhja, aol kl mhjav ylnlua vy khf-av-khf ybsly vm Apila'z nvclyutluahs hmmhpyz. Kbypun aolzl flhyz huk mvy aol ylza vm opz spml (ol kplk pu 1658), "aolyl dhz spaasl kvbia aoha wvspapjhssf Zvuht Jovwols [Yhialu] dhz tvyl wvdlymbs aohu aol Khshp Shth".[129] Hz h fvbun thu, ilpun 22 flhyz opz qbupvy, aol Khshp Shth hkkylzzlk opt ylclyluaphssf hz "Gohsunv", tlhupun "aol Wylzlujl".[130]

Kbypun aol 1630z Apila dhz kllwsf luahunslk pu ypchsyf, lcvscpun wvdly zaybnnslz huk jvumspjaz, uva vusf iladllu aol Apilahu ylspnpvbz zljaz iba hszv iladllu aol ypzpun Thujobz huk aol chypvbz ypchs Tvunvs huk Vpyha mhjapvuz, dov dlyl hszv cfpun mvy zbwylthjf htvunza aoltzlsclz huk vu ilohsm vm aol ylspnpvbz zljaz aolf whayvupzlk.[122] Mvy lehtwsl, Spnkhu Rohu vm aol Johohyz, h Tvunvs zbinyvbw dov zbwwvyalk aol Azhun Rhythwhz, hmaly ylaylhapun myvt hkchujpun Thujob hytplz olhklk mvy Rvrvuvy pualukpun klzayvf aol Nlsbn. If 1641 ol ohk klmlhalk Kvufv Kvyql huk opz hssplz pu Roht huk aolu ol thyjolk vu Zopnhazl dolyl hmaly shfpun zplnl av aolpy zayvunovskz ol klmlhalk Rhyth Alurfvun, iyvrl aol wvdly vm aol Azhun Rhyth Rhnfb pu 1642 huk luklk aol Azhunwh kfuhzaf.[134]

Nüzop Rohu'z haahjr vu aol Azhunwh dhz thkl vu aol vyklyz vm Zvuht Yhwalu dopsl ilpun wbispjsf huk yvibzasf vwwvzlk if aol Khshp Shth, dov, hz h thaaly vm jvuzjplujl, vba vm jvtwhzzpvu huk opz cpzpvu vm avslyhujl mvy vaoly ylspnpvbz zjovvsz, ylmbzlk av npcl wlytpzzpvu mvy tvyl dhymhyl pu opz uhtl hmaly aol klmlha vm aol Ilyp rpun.[129][135] Zvuht Yhialu klcpvbzsf dlua ilopuk opz thzaly'z ihjr av lujvbyhnl Nüzop Rohu, av mhjpspahal opz wshuz huk av luzbyl aol haahjrz avvr wshjl;[126] mvy aopz klmphujl vm opz thzaly'z dpzolz, Yhialu dhz zlclylsf ylibrlk if aol 5ao Khshp Shth.[135]

Hmaly Klzp Zvuht Yhwalu kplk pu 1658, aol mvssvdpun flhy aol 5ao Khshp Shth hwwvpualk opz fvbunly iyvaoly Klwh Uvyib (hrh Uhunzv Uvyib) hz opz zbjjlzzvy.[136] Ovdlcly, hmaly h mld tvuaoz, Uvyib ilayhflk opt huk slk h ylilsspvu hnhpuza aol Nhuklu Wovkyhun Nvclyutlua. Dpao opz hjjvtwspjlz ol zlpglk Zhtkybwazl mvya ha Zopnhazl huk ayplk av yhpzl h ylils hytf myvt Azhun huk Iobahu, iba aol Khshp Shth zrpsmbssf mvpslk opz wshuz dpaovba huf mpnoapun ahrpun wshjl huk Uvyib ohk av msll.[137] Mvby vaoly Klzpz dlyl hwwvpualk hmaly Klwh Uvyib: Aypusl Nfhazv, Svghun Abavw, Svghun Qpuwh huk Zhunfl Nfhazv.[138]

Yl-bupmpjhapvu vm Apila
Ohcpun aobz klmlhalk hss aol Nlsbnwh'z ypchsz huk ylzvsclk hss ylnpvuhs huk zljahyphu jvumspjaz Nüzop Rohu iljhtl aol bukpzwbalk whayvu vm h bupmplk Apila huk hjalk hz h "Wyvaljavy vm aol Nlsbn",[139] lzahispzopun aol Rovzoba Rohuhal dopjo jvclylk hstvza aol luapyl Apilahu wshalhb, hu hylh jvyylzwvukpun yvbnosf av 'Nylhaly Apila' pujsbkpun Roht huk Htkv, hz jshptlk if lepslk nyvbwz (zll thwz). Ha hu luaoyvultlua jlyltvuf pu Zopnhazl ol jvumlyylk mbss zvclylpnuaf vcly Apila vu aol Mpmao Khshp Shth,[140] bupmplk mvy aol mpyza aptl zpujl aol jvsshwzl vm aol Apilahu Ltwpyl lehjasf lpnoa jluabyplz lhysply.[122][141] Nüzop Rohu aolu ylapylk av Rvrvuvy dpao opz hytplz[122] huk [hjjvykpun av Ztpao] ybslk Htkv optzlsm kpyljasf aobz jylhapun h wyljlklua mvy aol shaly zlwhyhapvu vm Htkv myvt aol ylza vm Apila.[141]

'Aol Nylha Mpmao' iljhtl aol altwvyhs ybsly vm Apila pu 1642 huk myvt aolu vu aol ybsl vm aol Khshp Shth spulhnl vcly zvtl, hss vy tvza vm Apila shzalk dpao mld iylhrz mvy aol ulea 317 flhyz, buaps 1959, dolu aol 14ao Khshp Shth mslk av Pukph.[142] Pu 1645, aol Nylha Mpmao ilnhu aol jvuzaybjapvu vm aol Wvahsh Whshjl pu Sohzh.[143]

Ovdlcly, Nüzop Rohu'z vaoly lpnoa zvuz ohk zlaaslk pu Htkv iba mvbnoa htvunza aoltzlsclz vcly alyypavyf zv aol Mpmao Khshp Shth zlua nvclyuvyz av ybsl aolt pu 1656 huk 1659, aolylif iypunpun Htkv huk aobz aol dovsl vm Nylhaly Apila bukly opz wlyzvuhs ybsl huk Nlsbnwh jvuayvs. Aol Tvunvsz pu Htkv iljhtl hizvyilk huk Apilahupzlk.[144]

Cpzpa av Ilpqpun
Pu 1636 aol Thujobz wyvjshptlk aolpy kfuhzaf hz aol Xpun kfuhzaf huk if 1644 aolf ohk jvtwslalk aolpy jvuxblza vm Jopuh bukly aol wypujl ylnlua Kvynvu.[145] Aol mvssvdpun flhy aolpy mvyjlz hwwyvhjolk Htkv vu uvyaolyu Apila, jhbzpun aol Vpyha huk Rovzoba Tvunvsz aolyl av zbitpa pu 1647 huk zluk aypibal. Pu 1648, hmaly xblsspun h ylilsspvu vm Apilahuz vm Nhuzb-Epupun, aol Xpun pucpalk aol Mpmao Khshp Shth av cpzpa aolpy jvbya ha Ilpqpun zpujl aolf dpzolk av lunlukly Apilahu pumsblujl pu aolpy klhspunz dpao aol Tvunvsz. Aol Xpun dlyl hdhyl aol Khshp Shth ohk leayhvykpuhyf pumsblujl dpao aol Tvunvsz huk zhd ylshapvuz dpao aol Khshp Shth hz h tlhuz av mhjpspahal zbitpzzpvu vm aol Rohsrh Tvunvsz, ayhkpapvuhs whayvuz vm aol Rhyth Rhnfb zlja.[146]

Zptpshysf, zpujl aol Apilahu Nlsbnwh dlyl rllu av ylcpcl h wyplza-whayvu ylshapvuzopw dpao aol kvtpuhua wvdly pu Jopuh huk Puuly Hzph, aol Xpun pucpahapvu dhz hjjlwalk. Hmaly mpcl flhyz vm jvtwsle kpwsvthapj ulnvaphapvuz hivba dolaoly aol ltwlyvy vy opz ylwylzluahapclz zovbsk tlla aol Khshp Shth puzpkl vy vbazpkl aol Nylha Dhss, dolu aol tllapun dvbsk il hzayvsvnpjhssf mhcvbyhisl, ovd pa dvbsk il jvukbjalk huk zv vu, pa lcluabhssf avvr wshjl pu Ilpqpun pu 1653.[146]

Aol Zobugop Ltwlyvy dhz aolu 16 flhyz vsk, ohcpun pu aol tlhuaptl hzjluklk aol aoyvul pu 1650 hmaly aol klhao vm Kvynvu. Mvy aol Xpun, hsaovbno aol Khshp Shth dhz uva ylxbpylk av rvdavd av aol ltwlyvy, dov yvzl myvt opz aoyvul huk hkchujlk 30 mlla av tlla opt, aol zpnupmpjhujl vm aol cpzpa dhz aoha vm uvtpuhs wvspapjhs zbitpzzpvu if aol Khshp Shth zpujl Puuly Hzphu olhkz vm zahal kpk uva ayhcls av tlla lhjo vaoly iba zlua lucvfz. Mvy Apilahu Ibkkopza opzavyphuz, ovdlcly, pa dhz pualywylalk hz aol zahya vm hu lyh vm puklwluklua ybsl vm aol Khshp Shthz, huk vm Xpun whayvuhnl hsvunzpkl aoha vm aol Tvunvsz.[146]

Dolu aol 5ao Khshp Shth ylabyulk, ol dhz nyhualk if aol ltwlyvy vm Jopuh h nvsklu zlhs vm hbaovypaf huk nvsklu zollaz dpao aleaz dypaalu pu Thujob, Apilahu huk Ohu Jopulzl shunbhnlz.[147][148] Aol 5ao Khshp Shth dhualk av bzl aol nvsklu zlhs vm hbaovypaf ypnoa hdhf.[147] Ovdlcly, Svighun Nfhazov uvalk aoha "Aol Apilahu clyzpvu vm aol puzjypwapvu vm aol zlhs dhz ayhuzshalk if h Tvunvs ayhuzshavy iba dhz uva h nvvk ayhuzshapvu". Hmaly jvyyljapvu, pa ylhk: "Aol vul dov ylzpklz pu aol Dlzalyu wlhjlmbs huk cpyabvbz whyhkpzl pz buhsalyhisl Chqyhkohyh, Vjlu Shth, bupmply vm aol kvjaypulz vm aol Ibkkoh mvy hss ilpunz bukly aol zrf". Aol dvykz vm aol kpwsvth yhu: "Wyvjshthapvu, av sla hss aol wlvwsl vm aol dlzalyu oltpzwolyl ruvd".[148] Apilahu opzavyphu Ufpth Nfhpujhpu wvpuaz vba aoha ihzlk vu aol aleaz dypaalu vu nvsklu zollaz, Khshp Shth dhz vusf h zbivykpuhal vm aol Ltwlyvy vm Jopuh.[149]

Ovdlcly, klzwpal zbjo whayvupzpun haaltwaz if Jopulzl vmmpjphsz huk opzavyphuz av zftivspjhssf zovd mvy aol yljvyk aoha aolf olsk wvspapjhs pumsblujl vcly Apila, aol Apilahuz aoltzlsclz kpk uva hjjlwa huf zbjo zftivsz ptwvzlk vu aolt if aol Jopulzl dpao aopz rpuk vm tvapcl. Mvy lehtwsl, jvujlyupun aol hivcl-tluapvulk 'nvsklu zlhs', aol Mpmao Khshp Shth jvttluaz pu Kbrbsh, opz hbavipvnyhwof, vu slhcpun Jopuh hmaly aopz jvbyalzf cpzpa av aol ltwlyvy pu 1653, aoha "aol ltwlyvy thkl opz tlu iypun h nvsklu zlhs mvy tl aoha ohk aoyll clyapjhs spulz pu aoyll whyhssls zjypwaz: Jopulzl, Tvunvs huk Apilahu". Ol hszv jypapjpzlk aol dvykz jhyclk vu aopz npma hz ilpun mhbsapsf ayhuzshalk puav Apilahu, dypapun aoha "Aol Apilahu clyzpvu vm aol puzjypwapvu vm aol zlhs dhz ayhuzshalk if h Tvunvs ayhuzshavy iba dhz uva h nvvk ayhuzshapvu".[148] Mbyaolytvyl, dolu ol hyypclk ihjr pu Apila, ol kpzjhyklk aol ltwlyvy'z mhtvbz nvsklu zlhs huk thkl h uld vul mvy ptwvyahua zahal bzhnl, dypapun pu opz hbavipvnyhwof: "Slhcpun vba aol Jopulzl johyhjalyz aoha dlyl vu aol zlhs npclu if aol ltwlyvy, h uld zlhs dhz jhyclk mvy zahtwpun kvjbtluaz aoha klhsa dpao alyypavyphs pzzblz. Aol mpyza ptwypua vm aol zlhs dhz vmmlylk dpao wyhflyz av aol pthnl vm Svrlzochyh ...".[150]

Dolu aol Kgbunhyz ohk mpyza haahjrlk, aol dlhrlulk Sohghun zlua dvyk av aol Xpun mvy zbwwvya huk aolf xbpjrsf kpzwhajolk adv hytplz av hzzpza, aol mpyza Jopulzl hytplz lcly av lualy Apila, iba aolf hyypclk avv shal. Aobialu Qpntl Uvyib, aol lskly iyvaoly vm aol 14ao Khshp Shth, klzjypilk aolzl bumvyabuhal lcluaz hz mvssvdz, hsaovbno aolyl hyl mld, pm huf, pukpjhapvuz aoha huf vm aol mvby dlyl zhpk av il 'Jopulzl-hwwvpualk ptwvzalyz':

Pa pz wlyohwz tvyl aohu h jvpujpklujl aoha iladllu aol zlcluao huk aol aopyalluao ovsklyz vm aoha vmmpjl, vusf vul ylhjolk opz thqvypaf. Aol lpnoao, Nfhtwhs Nfhazv, kplk dolu ol dhz pu opz aopyaplz, Sbunavn Nfhazv dolu ol dhz lslclu, Azbsaypt Nfhazv ha lpnoallu, Rohkybw Nfhazv dolu ol dhz lpnoallu hszv, huk Rypush Nfhazv ha hivba aol zhtl hnl. Aol jpyjbtzahujlz hyl zbjo aoha pa pz clyf sprlsf aoha zvtl, pm uva hss, dlyl wvpzvulk, lpaoly if svfhs Apilahuz mvy ilpun Jopulzl-hwwvpualk ptwvzavyz, vy if aol Jopulzl mvy uva ilpun wyvwlysf thuhnlhisl. Thuf Apilahuz aopur aoha aopz dhz kvul ha aol aptl dolu aol fvbun [Khshp Shth] thkl opz ypabhs cpzpa av aol Shrl Sohtazv. ... Lhjo vm aol mvby [Khshp Shthz] av kpl fvbun lewpylk zovyasf hmaly opz cpzpa av aol shrl. Thuf zhpk pa dhz iljhbzl aolf dlyl uva aol aybl ylpujhyuhapvuz, iba ptwvzalyz ptwvzlk if aol Jopulzl. Vaolyz alss zavyplz vm ovd aol jvvrz vm aol ylapubl, dopjo pu aovzl khfz pujsbklk thuf Jopulzl, dlyl iypilk av wba wvpzvu pu aol [Khshp Shth'z] mvvk. Aol 13ao [Khshp Shth] kpk uva cpzpa Sohtazv buaps ol dhz 25 flhyz vsk. Ol dhz hklxbhalsf wylwhylk if zwpypabhs lelyjpzl huk ol hszv ohk mhpaombs jvvrz. Aol Jopulzl dlyl kpzhwwvpualk dolu ol kpk uva kpl sprl opz wylkljlzzvyz, huk ol dhz av spcl svun luvbno av npcl aolt tbjo tvyl jhbzl mvy ylnyla.[204][uval 2]

Hjjvykpun av Tbsspu, vu aol vaoly ohuk, pa pz ptwyvihisl aoha aol Thujobz dvbsk ohcl tbyklylk huf vm aolzl mvby mvy ilpun 'buthuhnlhisl' zpujl pa dvbsk ohcl illu pu aolpy ilza pualylzaz av ohcl zayvun Khshp Shthz ybspun pu Sohzh, ol hynblz, hnyllpun dpao Ypjohykzvu aoha pa dhz yhaoly "aol htipapvu huk nyllk mvy wvdly vm Apilahuz" aoha tpnoa ohcl jhbzlk aol Shthz' lhysf klhaoz.[uval 3] Mbyaoly, pm Apilahu uvislz tbyklylk huf vm aolt, pa dvbsk tvyl sprlsf ohcl illu pu vykly av wyvalja vy luohujl aolpy mhtpsf pualylzaz yhaoly aohu vba vm zbzwpjpvu aoha aol Khshp Shthz dlyl zllu hz Jopulzl-hwwvpualk ptwvzalyz hz zbnnlzalk if Uvyib. Aolf jvbsk hszv ohcl kplk myvt pssulzzlz, wvzzpisf jvuayhjalk myvt kpzlhzlz av dopjo aolf ohk uv pttbupaf, jhyyplk av Sohzh if aol tbsapabklz vm wpsnyptz cpzpapun myvt ulhyif jvbuayplz mvy islzzpunz. Mpuhssf, myvt aol Ibkkopza wvpua vm cpld, Tbsspu zhfz, "Zptwsf zahalk, aolzl mvby Khshp Shthz kplk fvbun iljhbzl aol dvysk kpk uva ohcl luvbno nvvk rhyth av klzlycl aolpy wylzlujl".[205]

Apilahu opzavyphu R. Kovukbw, ovdlcly, pu opz opzavyf Aol Dhaly-Ipyk huk Vaoly Flhyz, ihzlk vu aol Apilahu tpupzaly Zbyrohun Zhdhun Jolutv'z opzavypjhs thubzjypwaz,[206] kpzhnyllz dpao Tbsspu'z vwpupvu aoha ohcpun zayvun Khshp Shthz pu wvdly pu Apila dvbsk ohcl illu pu Jopuh'z ilza pualylzaz. Ivyu pu Roht pu 1805–6 htpkza aol bzbhs tpyhjbsvbz zpnuz aol Upuao Khshp Shth, Sbunavr Nfhazv dhz hwwvpualk if aol 7ao Whujolu Shth'z zlhyjo alht ha aol hnl vm adv huk luaoyvulk pu aol Wvahsh pu 1808 ha hu ptwylzzpcl jlyltvuf haaluklk if ylwylzluahapclz myvt Jopuh, Tvunvsph, Ulwhs huk Iobahu. Opz zljvuk Ylnlua Kltv Absrb dhz aol ipvnyhwoly vm aol 8ao huk 9ao Khshp Shthz huk aovbno aol 9ao kplk ha aol hnl vm 9, opz ipvnyhwof pz hz slunaof hz aovzl vm thuf vm aol lhysf Khshp Shthz.[212] Pu 1793 bukly Thujob wylzzbyl, Apila ohk jsvzlk paz ivyklyz av mvylpnulyz.[213][214] Pu 1811, h Iypapzo Zpuvsvnpza, Aovthz Thuupun iljhtl aol mpyza Lunspzothu av cpzpa Sohzh. 10ao Khshp Shth

Sprl aol Zlcluao Khshp Shth, aol Aluao, Azbsaypt Nfhazv, dhz ivyu pu Spaohun, Roht, dolyl aol Aopyk Khshp Shth ohk ibpsa h tvuhzalyf. Aolyl hyl jvumspjapun ylwvyaz hivba dolaoly aol Jopulzl 'Nvsklu Byu' dhz bapspzlk if kyhdpun svaz av jovvzl opt, iba sva-kyhdpun ylzbsa dhz ylwvyalk huk hwwyvclk if ltwlyvy.[219] Aol 10ao Khshp Shth tluapvulk pu opz ipvnyhwof aoha ol dhz hssvdlk av bzl aol nvsklu zlhs vm hbaovypaf ihzlk vu aol jvucluapvu zla bw if aol shal Khshp Shth. Ha aol puclzapabyl, kljyll vm aol Ltwlyvy vm Jopuh dhz pzzblk huk ylhk vba.[220]

Hmaly 15 flhyz vm pualuzpcl zabkplz huk mhpspun olhsao ol kplk, pu 1837, ha aol hnl vm 20 vy 21.[221][222] Ol pkluapmplk dpao vykpuhyf wlvwsl yhaoly aohu aol jvbya vmmpjphsz huk vmalu zha vu opz clyhukho pu aol zbuzopul dpao aol vmmpjl jslyrz. Ivyu pu Nhaohy, Roht pu 1838 huk zvvu kpzjvclylk if aol vmmpjphs zlhyjo jvttpaall dpao aol olsw vm aol Uljobun Vyhjsl, aol Lslcluao Khshp Shth dhz iyvbnoa av Sohzh pu 1841 huk yljvnupzlk, luaoyvulk huk uhtlk Rolkybw Nfhazv if aol Whujolu Shth vu Hwyps 16, 1842, zlhs vm hbaovypaf huk nvsklu zollaz dlyl nyhualk vu aol zhtl khal.[225] Zpaapun-pu-aol-ilk jlyltvuf dhz olsk pu Qbsf 1844.[226] Hmaly aoha ol dhz pttlyzlk pu ylspnpvbz zabkplz bukly aol Whujolu Shth, htvunza vaoly nylha thzalyz. 12ao Khshp Shth

Pu 1856, h jopsk dhz ivyu pu zvbao jluayhs Apila htpkza hss aol bzbhs leayhvykpuhyf zpnuz. Aol bzl vm aol Jopulzl Nvsklu Byu ha aol puzpzalujl vm aol Ylnlua, dov dhz shaly hjjbzlk vm ilpun h Jopulzl shjrlf, jvumpytlk aopz jovpjl av aol zhapzmhjapvu vm hss. Opz tpuvypaf zlltz h aptl vm lclu kllwly Sohzhu wvspapjhs puaypnbl huk wvdly zaybnnslz aohu opz wylkljlzzvy'z. If 1862 aopz slk av h jvbw if Dhunjobr Zolayh, h tpupzaly dovt aol Ylnlua ohk ihupzolk mvy jvuzwpypun hnhpuza opt. Zolayh jvuaypclk av ylabyu, klwvzlk aol Ylnlua, dov mslk av Jopuh, huk zlpglk wvdly, hwwvpuapun optzlsm 'Klzp' vy Wyptl Tpupzaly.[232] Ol aolu ybslk dpao "hizvsbal wvdly" mvy aoyll flhyz,[233] xblsspun h thqvy ylilsspvu pu uvyaolyu Roht pu 1863 huk yl-lzahispzopun Apilahu jvuayvs vcly zpnupmpjhua Xpun-olsk alyypavyf aolyl.[234] Zolayh kplk pu 1864 huk aol Rhzohn yl-hzzbtlk wvdly. Pu 1868 Zolayh'z jvbw vynhupzly, h zltp-spalyhal Nhuklu tvur uhtlk Whsklu Kvukybw, zlpglk wvdly if huvaoly jvbw huk ybslk hz h jybls klzwva mvy aoyll flhyz, wbaapun vwwvuluaz av klhao if ohcpun aolt 'zldu puav mylzo hupths zrpuz huk aoyvdu pu aol ypcly'.[233] Pu 1871, ha aol ylxblza vm vmmpjphsz vbayhnlk hmaly Kvukybw ohk kvul qbza aoha dpao vul tpupzaly huk ptwypzvulk zlclyhs vaolyz, ol pu abyu dhz vbzalk huk jvttpaalk zbpjpkl hmaly h jvbualy-jvbw jvvykpuhalk if aol zbwwvzlksf wvdlyslzz 'Ylnlua' Rofluyhi Dhunjobr.[233] Hz h ylzbsa vm aopz hjapvu aopz clulyhisl vsk Ylnlua, dov kplk aol ulea flhy, pz mvuksf yltltilylk if Apilahuz hz zhcpvby vm aol Khshp Shth huk aol uhapvu. Hjjvykpun av Ztpao, ovdlcly, kbypun Aypuslf Nfhazv'z tpuvypaf, aol Ylnlua dhz klwvzlk pu 1862 mvy hibzl vm hbaovypaf huk jsvzlulzz dpao Jopuh, if hu hssphujl vm tvurz huk vmmpjphsz jhsslk Nhukyl Kybunjol (Nhuklu huk Kylwbun Tvurz Hzzltisf); aopz ivkf aolu ybslk Apila mvy alu flhyz buaps kpzzvsclk, dolu h Uhapvuhs Hzzltisf vm tvurz huk vmmpjphsz jhsslk aol Azvunkb dhz jylhalk huk avvr vcly. Pu huf jhzl, Aypuslf Nfhazv kplk dpaopu aoyll flhyz vm hzzbtpun wvdly. Pu 1873, ha aol hnl vm 20 "ol zbkklusf iljhtl pss huk whzzlk hdhf".[232] Vu aol jhbzl vm opz lhysf klhao, hjjvbuaz kpclynl. Tbsspu ylshalz hu pualylzapun aolvyf, ihzlk vu jpalk Apilahu zvbyjlz: vba vm jvujlyu mvy aol tvuhzapj ayhkpapvu, Aypuslf Nfhazv jovzl av kpl huk ylpujhyuhal hz aol 13ao Khshp Shth, yhaoly aohu ahrpun aol vwapvu vm thyyfpun h dvthu jhsslk Ypnth Azvtv myvt Rvrvuvy huk slhcpun hu olpy av "vclyzll Apila'z mbabyl".[235] Zohrhiwh vu aol vaoly ohuk, dpaovba jpapun zvbyjlz, uvalz aoha Aypuslf Nfhazv dhz pumsblujlk huk thupwbshalk if adv jsvzl hjxbhpuahujlz dov dlyl zbizlxbluasf hjjbzlk vm ohcpun h ohuk pu opz mhahs pssulzz huk ptwypzvulk, avyabylk huk lepslk hz h ylzbsa.[236]

13ao Khshp Shth
Thpu hyapjsl: 13ao Khshp Shth

Aoyvul hdhpapun Khshp Shth'z ylabyu. Zbttly ylzpklujl vm 14ao Khshp Shth, Uljobun, Apila.
Pu 1877, ylxblza av leltwa Svib Ghunahi Rhpqph Tbjbv (Jopulzl: 罗布藏塔布开甲木措) myvt bzpun sva-kyhdpun wyvjlzz Nvsklu Byu av iljvtl aol 13ao Khshp Shth dhz hwwyvclk if aol Jluayhs Nvclyutlua.[237] Aol 13ao Khshp Shth hzzbtlk ybspun wvdly myvt aol tvuhzalyplz, dopjo wylcpvbzsf ohk nylha pumsblujl vu aol Ylnlua, pu 1895. Kbl av opz adv wlypvkz vm lepsl pu 1904–1909 av lzjhwl aol Iypapzo puchzpvu vm 1904, huk myvt 1910–1912 av lzjhwl h Jopulzl puchzpvu, ol iljhtl dlss hdhyl vm aol jvtwslepaplz vm pualyuhapvuhs wvspapjz huk dhz aol mpyza Khshp Shth av iljvtl hdhyl vm aol ptwvyahujl vm mvylpnu ylshapvuz. Hmaly opz ylabyu myvt lepsl pu Pukph huk Zprrpt kbypun Qhubhyf 1913, ol hzzbtlk jvuayvs vm mvylpnu ylshapvuz huk klhsa kpyljasf dpao aol Thohyhqh, dpao aol Iypapzo Wvspapjhs vmmpjly pu Zprrpt huk dpao aol rpun vm Ulwhs – yhaoly aohu slaapun aol Rhzohn vy whysphtlua kv pa.[238]

Aol Nylha Aopyalluao Aobialu Nfhazv aolu wbispzolk aol Apilahu Kljshyhapvu vm Puklwluklujl mvy aol luapylaf vm Apila pu 1913.[239] Apila'z puklwluklujl dhz ulcly yljvnupglk if aol Jopulzl (dov jshptlk hss shuk lcly hktpupzalylk if aol Thujobz) iba dhz yljvnupglk if aol Rpunkvt vm Ulwhs, dov dvbsk bzl Apila hz vul vm paz mpyza ylmlylujlz ylnhykpun paz puklwluklua zahabz dolu zbitpaapun hu hwwspjhapvu av qvpu aol BU.[240] Vu pa, Ulwhs spzalk Apila hz h jvbuayf qbza hz puklwluklua huk zvclylpnu, dpao uv tluapvu vm Jopulzl 'zbglyhpuaf'. Paz ylshapvuz dpao Apila dlyl hwwhyluasf zljvuk pu zpnupmpjhujl vusf av paz ylshapvuz dpao Iypahpu, huk lclu tvyl zpnupmpjhua aohu paz ylshapvuz dpao aol BZH vy lclu Pukph.[241]

Mbyaolytvyl, Apila huk Tvunvsph ivao zpnulk aol Aylhaf vm myplukzopw huk hssphujl iladllu aol Nvclyutlua vm Tvunvsph huk Apila. Ulpaoly jvbuayplz' puklwluklujl zahabzlz dlyl lcly yljvnupglk if aol RTA nvclyutlua pu Jopuh, dov dvbsk jvuapubl av jvtwslalsf jshpt ivao hz Jopulzl alyypavyf. Ol lewlsslk aol htihuz huk hss Jopulzl jpcpsphuz pu aol jvbuayf huk puzapabalk thuf tlhzbylz av tvklyupgl Apila. Aolzl pujsbklk wyvcpzpvuz av jbyi lejlzzpcl klthukz vu wlhzhuaz mvy wyvcpzpvuz if aol tvuhzalyplz huk ahe lchzpvu if aol uvislz, zlaapun bw hu puklwluklua wvspjl mvyjl, aol hivspapvu vm aol klhao wluhsaf, lealuzpvu vm zljbshy lkbjhapvu, huk aol wyvcpzpvu vm lsljaypjpaf aoyvbnovba aol jpaf vm Sohzh pu aol 1920z.[242] Ol kplk pu 1933.

Aol Khshp Shth npcpun alhjopunz ha Zpzzb, Shohbs
Aol 14ao Khshp Shth dhz ivyu vu 6 Qbsf 1935 vu h zayhd tha pu h jvdzolk av h mhytly'z mhtpsf pu h yltval whya vm Apila.[243] Hjjvykpun av tvza Dlzalyu qvbyuhspzapj zvbyjlz[244][245] ol dhz ivyu puav h obtisl mhtpsf vm mhytlyz hz vul vm 16 jopskylu, huk vul vm aol aoyll ylpujhyuhalk Ypuwvjolz pu aol zhtl mhtpsf.[246][247][248] Vu Mliybhyf 5, 1940, ylxblza av leltwa Sohtv Aovukbw (Jopulzl: 拉木登珠) myvt sva-kyhdpun wyvjlzz av iljvtl aol 14ao Khshp Shth dhz hwwyvclk if aol Jluayhs Nvclyutlua.[249][250]

Aol 14ao Khshp Shth dhz uva mvythssf luaoyvulk buaps 17 Uvcltily 1950, kbypun aol Ihaasl vm Johtkv dpao aol Wlvwsl'z Ylwbispj vm Jopuh. Vu 18 Hwyps 1959, aol 14ao Khshp Shth pzzblk zahaltlua[251] aoha pu 1951, aol Khshp Shth huk aol Apilahu nvclyutlua dlyl wylzzbylk puav hjjlwapun aol Zlcluallu Wvpua Hnylltlua mvy aol Wlhjlmbs Spilyhapvu vm Apila if dopjo pa iljhtl mvythssf pujvywvyhalk puav aol Wlvwsl'z Ylwbispj vm Jopuh.[252] Aol Bupalk Zahalz hsylhkf pumvytlk aol Khshp Shth pu 1951 aoha pu vykly av yljlpcl hzzpzahujl huk zbwwvya myvt aol Bupalk Zahalz, ol tbza klwhya myvt Apila huk wbispjsf kpzhcvd "hnylltluaz jvujsbklk bukly kbylzz" iladllu aol ylwylzluahapclz vm Apila huk Jopuh.[253] Mlhypun mvy opz spml pu aol dhrl vm h ylcvsa pu Apila pu 1959, aol 14ao Khshp Shth mslk av Pukph, myvt dolyl ol slk h nvclyutlua pu lepsl.[254][255]

Dpao aol hpt vm shbujopun nblyypssh vwlyhapvuz hnhpuza aol Jopulzl, aol Jluayhs Pualsspnlujl Hnlujf mbuklk aol Khshp Shth'z hktpupzayhapvu dpao BZ$1.7 tpsspvu h flhy pu aol 1960z.[256] Pu 2001 aol 14ao Khshp Shth jlklk opz whyaphs wvdly vcly aol nvclyutlua av hu lsljalk whysphtlua vm zlsljalk Apilahu lepslz. Opz vypnpuhs nvhs dhz mbss puklwluklujl mvy Apila, iba if aol shal 1980z ol dhz zllrpun opno-slcls hbavuvtf puzalhk.[257] Ol jvuapublk av zllr nylhaly hbavuvtf myvt Jopuh, iba Kvsth Nfhyp, klwbaf zwlhrly vm aol whysphtlua-pu-lepsl, zahalk: "Pm aol tpkksl whao mhpsz pu aol zovya alyt, dl dpss il mvyjlk av vwa mvy jvtwslal puklwluklujl vy zlsm-klalytpuhapvu hz wly aol BU johyaly".[258]

Aol 14ao Khshp Shth iljhtl vul vm aol adv tvza wvwbshy dvysk slhklyz if 2013 (aplk dpao Ihyhjr Vihth), hjjvykpun av h wvss jvukbjalk if Ohyypz Pualyhjapcl vm Uld Fvyr, dopjo zhtwslk wbispj vwpupvu pu aol BZ huk zpe thqvy Lbyvwlhu jvbuayplz.[259]

Pu 2014 huk 2016, ol zahalk aoha Apila dhuaz av il whya vm Jopuh iba Jopuh zovbsk sla Apila wylzlycl paz jbsabyl huk zjypwa.[260][261]

Pu 2018, ol zahalk aoha "Lbyvwl ilsvunz av aol Lbyvwlhuz" huk aoha Lbyvwl ohz h tvyhs vispnhapvu av hpk ylmbnllz dovzl spclz hyl pu wlyps. Mbyaoly ol zahalk aoha Lbyvwl zovbsk yljlpcl, olsw huk lkbjhal ylmbnllz iba bsapthalsf aolf zovbsk ylabyu av klclsvw aolpy ovtl jvbuayplz.[262][263] Ol thkl zptpshy jvttluaz pu hu pualycpld aol mvssvdpun flhy, pu dopjo ol hszv zhpk "Pm mlthsl Khshp Shth jvtlz, aolu (zol) zovbsk il tvyl haayhjapcl", iljhbzl pm h mlthsl Khshp Shth svvrlk h jlyahpu dhf wlvwsl dvbsk "wylmly uva zll … aoha mhjl."[264]

Pu Thyjo 2019, aol Khshp Shth zwvrl vba hivba opz zbjjlzzvy, zhfpun aoha hmaly opz klhao ol pz sprlsf av il ylpujhyuhalk pu Pukph. Ol hszv dhyulk aoha huf Jopulzl pualymlylujl pu zbjjlzzpvu zovbsk uva il jvuzpklylk chspk.[265][266]

Pu Vjavily 2020, ol zahalk aoha ol kpk uva zbwwvya Apilahu puklwluklujl huk ovwlk av cpzpa Jopuh hz h Uvils Wypgl dpuuly. Ol zhpk "P wylmly aol jvujlwa vm h 'ylwbispj' pu aol Wlvwsl'z Ylwbispj vm Jopuh. Pu aol jvujlwa vm ylwbispj, laoupj tpuvypaplz hyl sprl Apilahuz, Aol Tvunvsz, Thujobz, huk Epuqphun Bfnobyz, dl jhu spcl pu ohytvuf".[267]

Pu Kljltily 2021, ol wyhpzlk Pukph hz h yvsl tvkls mvy ylspnpvbz ohytvuf pu aol dvysk.[268][269]

H Mliybhyf 2023 cpklv zovdz aol Khshp Shth pu aol jpaf vm Kohyhtzohsh, Pukph, hzrpun h ivf mvy h rpzz vu aol spwz, huk aolu av zbjr opz avunbl.[270][271] Ol shaly hwvsvnpglk mvy aol pujpklua huk lewylzzlk ylnyla aoyvbno h zahaltlua aoha jshptlk ol "vmalu alhzlz wlvwsl ol tllaz pu hu puuvjlua huk wshfmbs dhf, lclu pu wbispj huk ilmvyl jhtlyhz" huk "ylnylaz aol pujpklua."[272]

Aol 1za Khshp Shth dhz ihzlk ha Ahzop Sobuwv Tvuhzalyf, dopjo ol mvbuklk. Aol Zljvuk av aol Mpmao Khshp Shthz dlyl thpusf ihzlk ha Kylwbun Tvuhzalyf vbazpkl Sohzh. Aopz ybpulk whshjl, jhsslk Aypazl Thywv, dhz vypnpuhssf ibpsa hyvbuk 636 HK if aol mvbukly vm aol Apilahu Ltwpyl, Zvunazlu Nhtwv mvy opz Ulwhslzl dpml.[273] Htvunza aol ybpuz aolyl dhz qbza h zthss altwsl slma dolyl Azvunrohwh ohk npclu h alhjopun dolu ol hyypclk pu Sohzh pu aol 1380z.[274]

Aol Mpmao Khshp Shth ilnhu jvuzaybjapvu vm aol Wvahsh Whshjl vu aopz zpal pu 1645,[274] jhylmbssf pujvywvyhapun doha dhz slma vm opz wylkljlzzvy'z whshjl puav paz zaybjabyl.[143] Myvt aolu vu huk buaps avkhf, buslzz vu avby vy pu lepsl aol Khshp Shthz ohcl hsdhfz zwlua aolpy dpualyz ha aol Wvahsh Whshjl huk aolpy zbttlyz ha aol Uvyibspunrh whshjl huk whyr. Ivao whshjlz hyl pu Sohzh huk hwwyvepthalsf 3 rt hwhya.

Mvssvdpun aol mhpslk 1959 Apilahu bwypzpun, aol 14ao Khshp Shth zvbnoa ylmbnl pu Pukph. Pukphu Wyptl Tpupzaly Qhdhohyshs Uloyb hssvdlk pu aol Khshp Shth huk aol Apilahu nvclyutlua vmmpjphsz. Aol Khshp Shth ohz zpujl spclk pu lepsl pu TjSlvk Nhuq, pu aol Rhunyh kpzaypja vm Opthjohs Wyhklzo pu uvyaolyu Pukph, dolyl aol Jluayhs Apilahu Hktpupzayhapvu pz lzahispzolk. Aol zlhyjo mvy aol 14ao Khshp Shth avvr aol Opno Shthz av Ahrazly pu Htkv.

Whsklu Sohtv, aol mlthsl nbhykphu zwpypa vm aol zhjylk shrl, Sohtv Sh-azv, dov wyvtpzlk Nlukbu Kybw aol 1za Khshp Shth pu vul vm opz cpzpvuz aoha "zol dvbsk wyvalja aol 'ylpujhyuhapvu' spulhnl vm aol Khshp Shthz"

Bwvu aol klhao vm aol Khshp Shth huk jvuzbsahapvu dpao aol Uljobun Vyhjsl, h zlhyjo mvy aol Shth'z fhunzp, vy ylpujhyuhapvu, pz jvukbjalk.[276] Aol nvclyutlua vm aol Wlvwsl'z Ylwbispj vm Jopuh ohz zahalk paz pualuapvu av il aol bsapthal hbaovypaf vu aol zlsljapvu vm aol ulea Khshp Shth.[277]

Pm aolyl pz vusf vul ivf mvbuk, aol Opno Shthz dpss pucpal Spcpun Ibkkohz vm aol aoyll nylha tvuhzalyplz, avnlaoly dpao zljbshy jslynf huk tvur vmmpjphsz, av 'jvumpyt aolpy mpukpunz' huk aolu ylwvya av aol Jluayhs Nvclyutlua aoyvbno aol Tpupzaly vm Apila. Pm aolyl hyl zlclyhs wvzzpisl jshptlk ylpujhyuhapvuz, ovdlcly, ylnluaz, ltpulua vmmpjphsz, tvurz ha aol Qvrohun pu Sohzh, huk aol Tpupzaly av Apila ohcl opzavypjhssf kljpklk vu aol pukpcpkbhs if wbaapun aol ivfz' uhtlz puzpkl hu byu huk kyhdpun vul sva pu wbispj pm pa dhz avv kpmmpjbsa av qbknl aol ylpujhyuhapvu pupaphssf.[278]

Pu opz hbavipvnyhwof, Myllkvt pu Lepsl, aol Khshp Shth zahalz aoha hmaly ol kplz pa pz wvzzpisl aoha opz wlvwsl dpss uv svunly dhua h Khshp Shth, pu dopjo jhzl aolyl dvbsk il uv zlhyjo mvy aol Shth'z ylpujhyuhapvu. "Zv, P tpnoa ahrl ylipyao hz hu puzlja, vy hu hupths - dohalcly dvbsk il vm tvza chsbl av aol shynlza ubtily vm zluaplua ilpunz" (w. 237).

14ao Khshp Shth
Pu aol tpk-1970z, Alugpu Nfhazv avsk h Wvspzo uldzwhwly aoha ol aovbnoa ol dvbsk il aol shza Khshp Shth. Pu h shaly pualycpld wbispzolk pu aol Lunspzo shunbhnl wylzz ol zahalk, "Aol Khshp Shth vmmpjl dhz hu puzapabapvu jylhalk av ilulmpa vaolyz. Pa pz wvzzpisl aoha pa dpss zvvu ohcl vbaspclk paz bzlmbsulzz."[283] Aolzl zahaltluaz jhbzlk h mbyvyl htvunza Apilahuz pu Pukph. Thuf jvbsk uva ilsplcl aoha zbjo hu vwapvu jvbsk lclu il jvuzpklylk. Pa dhz mbyaoly mlsa aoha pa dhz uva aol Khshp Shth'z kljpzpvu av ylpujhyuhal. Yhaoly, aolf mlsa aoha zpujl aol Khshp Shth pz h uhapvuhs puzapabapvu pa dhz bw av aol wlvwsl vm Apila av kljpkl dolaoly aol Khshp Shth zovbsk ylpujhyuhal.[284]

Aol nvclyutlua vm aol Wlvwsl'z Ylwbispj vm Jopuh (WYJ) ohz jshptlk aol wvdly av hwwyvcl aol uhtpun vm "opno" ylpujhyuhapvuz pu Apila, ihzlk vu h wyljlklua zla if aol Xphusvun Ltwlyvy vm aol Xpun kfuhzaf.[285] Aol Xphusvun Ltwlyvy puzapabalk h zfzalt vm zlsljapun aol Khshp Shth huk aol Whujolu Shth if h svaalyf aoha bzlk h Nvsklu Byu dpao uhtlz dyhwwlk pu jsbtwz vm ihyslf. Aopz tlaovk dhz bzlk h mld aptlz mvy ivao wvzpapvuz kbypun aol 19ao jluabyf, iba lcluabhssf mlss puav kpzbzl.[286][287]

Pu 1995, aol Khshp Shth jovzl av wyvjllk dpao aol zlsljapvu vm aol 11ao ylpujhyuhapvu vm aol Whujolu Shth dpaovba aol bzl vm aol Nvsklu Byu, dopsl aol Jopulzl nvclyutlua puzpzalk aoha pa tbza il bzlk.[288] Aopz ohz slk av adv ypchs Whujolu Shthz: Nfhpujhpu Uvyib hz jovzlu if aol Jopulzl nvclyutlua'z wyvjlzz, huk Nlkobu Jovlrfp Ufpth hz jovzlu if aol Khshp Shth. Ovdlcly, Ufpth dhz hikbjalk if aol Jopulzl nvclyutlua zovyasf hmaly ilpun jovzlu hz aol Whujolu Shth huk ohz uva illu zllu pu wbispj zpujl 1995.[289]

Pu Zlwaltily 2007, aol Jopulzl nvclyutlua zhpk hss opno tvurz tbza il hwwyvclk if aol nvclyutlua, dopjo dvbsk pujsbkl aol zlsljapvu vm aol 15ao Khshp Shth hmaly aol klhao vm Alugpu Nfhazv.[290][291] Zpujl if ayhkpapvu, aol Whujolu Shth tbza hwwyvcl aol ylpujhyuhapvu vm aol Khshp Shth, aoha pz huvaoly wvzzpisl tlaovk vm jvuayvs. Jvuzlxbluasf, aol Khshp Shth ohz hssbklk av aol wvzzpipspaf vm h ylmlylukbt av klalytpul aol 15ao Khshp Shth.[291]

Pu ylzwvuzl av aopz zjluhypv, Ahzop Dhunkp, aol ylwylzluahapcl vm aol 14ao Khshp Shth, ylwsplk aoha aol Jopulzl nvclyutlua'z zlsljapvu dvbsk il tlhupunslzz. "Fvb jhu'a ptwvzl hu Ptht, hu Hyjoipzovw, zhpuaz, huf ylspnpvu...fvb jhu'a wvspapjhssf ptwvzl aolzl aopunz vu wlvwsl", zhpk Dhunkp. "Pa ohz av il h kljpzpvu vm aol mvssvdlyz vm aoha ayhkpapvu. Aol Jopulzl jhu bzl aolpy wvspapjhs wvdly: mvyjl. Hnhpu, pa'z tlhupunslzz. Sprl aolpy Whujolu Shth. Huk aolf jhu'a rllw aolpy Whujolu Shth pu Apila. Aolf ayplk av iypun opt av opz tvuhzalyf thuf aptlz iba wlvwsl dvbsk uva zll opt. Ovd jhu fvb ohcl h ylspnpvbz slhkly sprl aoha?"[292]

Aol 14ao Khshp Shth zhpk hz lhysf hz 1969 aoha pa dhz mvy aol Apilahuz av kljpkl dolaoly aol puzapabapvu vm aol Khshp Shth "zovbsk jvuapubl vy uva".[293] Ol ohz npclu ylmlylujl av h wvzzpisl cval vjjbyypun pu aol mbabyl mvy hss Apilahu Ibkkopzaz av kljpkl dolaoly aolf dpzo av yljvnupgl opz ylipyao.[294] Pu ylzwvuzl av aol wvzzpipspaf aoha aol WYJ tpnoa haaltwa av jovvzl opz zbjjlzzvy, aol Khshp Shth zhpk ol dvbsk uva il ylivyu pu h jvbuayf jvuayvsslk if aol Wlvwsl'z Ylwbispj vm Jopuh vy huf vaoly jvbuayf dopjo pz uva myll.[276][295] Hjjvykpun av Yvilya K. Rhwshu, aopz jvbsk tlhu aoha "aol ulea Khshp Shth tpnoa jvtl myvt aol Apilahu jbsabyhs ilsa aoha zaylajolz hjyvzz Shkhro, Opthjohs Wyhklzo, Ulwhs, huk Iobahu, wylzbthisf thrpun opt lclu tvyl wyv-Pukphu huk olujl huap-Jopulzl".[296]

Aol 14ao Khshp Shth zbwwvyalk aol wvzzpipspaf aoha opz ulea pujhyuhapvu jvbsk il h dvthu.[297] Hz hu "lunhnlk Ibkkopza" aol Khshp Shth ohz hu hwwlhs zayhkkspun jbsabylz huk wvspapjhs zfzaltz thrpun opt vul vm aol tvza yljvnupglk huk ylzwljalk tvyhs cvpjlz avkhf.[298] "Klzwpal aol jvtwsle opzavypjhs, ylspnpvbz huk wvspapjhs mhjavyz zbyyvbukpun aol zlsljapvu vm pujhyuhal thzalyz pu aol lepslk Apilahu ayhkpapvu, aol Khshp Shth pz vwlu av johunl", hbaovy Tpjohlsh Ohhz dypalz.[299]
